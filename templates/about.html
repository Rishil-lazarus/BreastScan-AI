Purpose & Mission

BreastScan AI was developed to assist healthcare professionals in the early detection and classification of breast abnormalities from ultrasound scans. By leveraging deep learning, the system provides fast, accurate, and reliable predictions to support clinical decisions.

Early detection saves lives. Our mission is to provide a second-opinion system that empowers radiologists and clinicians by highlighting areas of concern that may require further review.

Classification Categories

BreastScan AI classifies ultrasound images into three categories:

Benign

Non-cancerous findings with no malignant features.

Malignant

Cancerous tissue requiring immediate medical evaluation.

Normal

Healthy breast tissue with no suspicious features.
